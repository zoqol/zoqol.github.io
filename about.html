[+] ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Javid, I studied a BA in Computer Engineering and then completed a Masters in Philosophical Logic. As a programmer I like physics and graphics programming,
computational geometry, algorithmic and generative arts.
Besides computer stuffs, I love Philosophy & Logic. I wrote my MA thesis on a neo-carnapian approach to ontology.
I see logic as a non-emty intersection of Philosophy, Mathematics & Computer Science.
Although I'm more inclined toward philosophy but trying to learn related things from all of these areas.
Like many logicians and philosophers, I'm fascinated by the idea of paraconsistency and contradiction.
Currently, I'm working on the expressive power of different proof systems of some paraconsistent logics over certain semantical structures. trying to extend the idea of natural semantics for $LFI's$, specifically da Costa's amazing hierarchy $C_0,C_1...C_\omega$. Inferentialism works as the main meaning theory and motivation for this program.

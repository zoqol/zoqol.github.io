Hello, I'm Javid. I have a bachelor's degree in software engineering, interested in visual programming, such as generative and algorithmic art.
In addition to my work in computers, I'm passionate about Metaphysics & Logic. I completed my master's degree working on the neo-Carnapians ontology.
At the beginning of 2025, I defended my PhD dissertation titled "Model-Theoretic Inferentialism, Non-determinism and Formal Inconsistency".
My areas of interest include the following concepts: existence, modality, truth, set, negation and inference.

Javid Jafari

Hi, I’m Javid, I’m a programmer who is fascinated by using code to makes things move. My main interests are creative coding, generative and algorithmic arts. (see my works here)
In addition to my work in computers, I’m also interested in philosophy (particularly, Metaphysics & Logic.).

I wrote my master’s thesis on a neo-Carnapian approach to ontology.

In early 2025, I completed my PhD dissertation entitled "Model-Theoretic Inferentialism, Non-determinism and Formal Inconsistency" under the supervision of Davood Hosseini.

My favorite concepts in metaphysics are: existence, modality, truth, set, negation and inference.

In logic, my research focus is on the relationship between paraconsistent and modal logics.
